Personas and Storyboarding

Ten One Tea House

Ten One Tea House is one of numerous boba shops on Brown University's campus. Many students and families frequent this establishment for a quick, refreshing beverage or dessert. In the shop is a kiosk powered by Snackpass that customers may use to order their food or drink. Below are two Personas representing two possible users for this interface as well as a storyboard. Before constructing these, I observed and interviewed users of this interface.

Description: Having first downloaded the Snackpass app, users select their desired menu items and specify their preferences (e.g. type of milk, amount of ice) and toppings of choice. After they are satisfied with their cart, they finalize their order, pay, and type their phone number for notifications. This is all done by touch screen. Their Snackpass order number will be displayed, and they will proceed to wait for their order to be completed.

Observations

Person 1

    College student. Very experienced with the interface. Listening to music on his earbuds. Goes in, orders, gets his drink, and leaves. Very fast and efficient.

Person 2

    College student. Here with a friend. Shows friend stuff on her phone. Scrolls through the interface, deciding between all allotted options. Struggles a little with the touch screen. Feels bad about not giving a tip.

Person 3

    Mother of two kids. Very experienced with the interface. On the phone. Asks her kids many clarifying questions. Takes a photo of her kids.

Interview Summary

How often do you come to Ten One?

  • Most users came here often, at least once a week.

Was ordering easy?

  • Users thought that ordering was very easy.
  • The lack of social interaction was a positive for some.
  • Buttons and overall flow were regarded as intuitive.

How long did it take for you to order?

  • Most users remarked that it did not take very long, at about a minute max.
  • Most of the time came from deciding what to order.

What's your least favorite thing about ordering?

  • Most users felt that ordering was quick and intuitive.
  • A first time user thought it was a bit annoying that they had to download Snackpass, as the register was not working that day.

Susie is representative of the users of the Ten One kiosk because many college students use boba shops as a social location when they want a break from school.

Multitasking Maria

Multitasking Maria is a busy mom who is taking her daughter to get a treat after going clothes shopping. Maria is quite experienced with the interface, though having to type in her phone number slows things down. Maria is representative of the users of the Ten One kiosk because young children love cold, sweet drinks, and they need parents to take them to get them. Boba shops are often a stop before or after some other errand(s).
